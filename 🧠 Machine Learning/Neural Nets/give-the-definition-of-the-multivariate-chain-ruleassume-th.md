## Back
\(\frac{d}{d t} f(x(t), y(t))=\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} \frac{d
x}{d t}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial y} \frac{d y}{d t}\)
<div>
  Note that \(\partial f\) has already been computed by the
  algorithm. And \({dt}\) is the expression to be evaluated.
</div>
<div>
  \(\bar{t}=\bar{x} \frac{d x}{d t}+\bar{y} \frac{d y}{d t}\)
