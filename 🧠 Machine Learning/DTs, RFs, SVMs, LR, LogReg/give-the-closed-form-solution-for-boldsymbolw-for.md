## Front
Give the closed-form solution for \(\boldsymbol{w}^{*}\) for the generalized linear regression.

## Back
\(\begin{aligned} \boldsymbol{w}^{*}=\left(\boldsymbol{\Phi}^{T}
\boldsymbol{\Phi}\right)^{-1} \boldsymbol{\Phi}^{T} \boldsymbol{y}
\\ \text { with } \boldsymbol{\Phi}=\left[\begin{array}{c}
\boldsymbol{\phi}_{1}^{T} \\ \vdots \\ \boldsymbol{\phi}_{n}^{T}
\end{array}\right] & \end{aligned}\)
<div>
  Data matrix is replaced by the basis function matrix.
