### Front
Explain what happens if the parameter \(C\) is small, large or infinite for SVMs.

### Back
<div>
<div><ul>
<li>Small \(C\). Constraints have little influence → large margin → large regularization</li>
<li>Large \(C\) Constraints have large influence → small margin → small regularization</li>
<li>\(C\) infinite: Constraints are enforced → hard margin → no regularization</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
