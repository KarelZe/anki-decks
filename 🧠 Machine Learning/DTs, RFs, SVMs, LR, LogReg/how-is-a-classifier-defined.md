## Front
<p>How is a <b>Classifier</b> defined?

## Back
<p>Given the dataset \(\mathcal{D}=\left\{\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{i},
c_{i}\right)\right\}_{i=1 \ldots N}\), where \(\boldsymbol{x}_{i}
\in \mathbb{R}^{d}\) are the input samples and \(c \in\{1 \ldots
K\}\) are the class labels, we want to learn a classifier \(f(x)\)
that predicts the class label for unseen samples.
<p>For \(K=2\) its binary classification and for \(K > 2\) its
multi-class classification.
