## Front
What happens if the slack variable \(\xi_{i} = 0\), \(0 \leq \xi_{i} \leq 1\) and \(\xi_{i}>1\)?

## Back
<div>
<div><ul>
<li>\(0 \leq \xi_{i} \leq 1\) sample is between margin and decision boundary: <b>margin violation</b></li>
<li>\(\xi_{i}>1\) sample is on the wrong side of the decision boundary: misclassified</li>
<li>\(\xi_{i}= 0\) sample is on the decision boundary</li>
