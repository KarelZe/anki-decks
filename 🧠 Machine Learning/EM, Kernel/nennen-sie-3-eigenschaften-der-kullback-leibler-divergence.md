## Back

  <div>
    <ul>
      <li>Its always non-negative: \(\mathrm{KL}(q \| p) \geq 0\)
      <li>If its zero, both distributions are the same:
      \(\mathrm{KL}(q \| p)=0 \Longleftrightarrow q=p\)
      <li>It is non-symmetric: \(\mathrm{KL}(q \| p) \neq
      \mathrm{KL}(p \| q)\)
    </ul>
