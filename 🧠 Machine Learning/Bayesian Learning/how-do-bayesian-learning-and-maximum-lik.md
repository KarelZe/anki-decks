### Front
How do <b>Bayesian Learning</b> and <b>Maximum Likelihood</b>
relate?

### Back
For large datasets, the posterior will be a point estimate, as
\(\lim _{n \rightarrow \infty} p\left(\boldsymbol{\theta} \mid
\mathcal{D}_{n}\right)=\delta\left(\boldsymbol{\theta}-\boldsymbol{\theta}^{*}\right)\)
<div>
  <b>Maximum Likelihood</b> and <b>Bayesian Learning</b> will be
  the same.
</div>
