### Front
What is the purpose of the <b>Prior</b> \(p(\theta)\) in
<b>Bayesian Learning</b>?
<div>
  Also, give an example.
</div>

### Back
The prior \(p(\theta)\) captures the belief and domain knowledge.
<div>
  Example e. g. Gaussian prior:
</div>
<div>
  \(p(\boldsymbol{\theta})=\mathcal{N}\left(\boldsymbol{\theta}
  \mid \mathbf{0}, \lambda^{-1} \boldsymbol{I}\right),\)
</div>
<div>
  where \(\lambda\) is the precision of the prior.
</div>
