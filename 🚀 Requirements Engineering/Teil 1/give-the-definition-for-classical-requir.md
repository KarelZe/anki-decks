### Front
Give the <b>definition</b> for <b>classical requirements
engineering</b>? What is it's <b>intuition</b>?

### Back
<b>Definition</b>: The application of a systematic, disciplined,
quantifiable approach to the specification and management of
requirements, that is the application of engineering to
requirements. <b>Intuition</b>: Upfront engineering <b>Goal</b>:
Complete, unamigious requirements prior to design
