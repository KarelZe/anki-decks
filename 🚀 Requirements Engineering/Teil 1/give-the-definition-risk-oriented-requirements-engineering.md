## Front
Give the definition <b>risk-oriented requirements engineering</b>.
What is the <b>intuition</b>? What is the <b>goal</b>?

## Back
<b>Definition:</b> Specifiying and managing requirements to
minimize the risk of delivering a system that does not meet the
stakeholders desires and needs. <b>Metaphor:</b> Balancing effort
and value (zero risk not possible) <b>Goal:</b> Mitigate risk.
