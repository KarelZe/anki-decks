### Front
Explain the principle of RE <b>value orientation</b>.

### Back
Requirements shall deliver value. Value is estimated from the
<b>benefit of reducing development risk</b> (i. e., the risk of not
meeting the stakeholders' desires and needs) - cost of
<b>specifiying the requirement.</b> Degree depends on risk of
project. Hence, high risk project: fully fledged RE.
