### Front
What is the <b>value</b> from specifying <b>requirements</b>?

### Back
<b>Value</b> is the <b>benefit</b> of an <b>explicit
specification</b> e. g., bringing down the probability of
developing a system that doesn't satisfy expectations and needs to
an acceptable level <b>minus</b> the <b>cost</b> of writing,
reading and maintaining a specification.
