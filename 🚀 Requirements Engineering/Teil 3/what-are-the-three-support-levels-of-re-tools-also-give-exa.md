## Back
<ul>
  <li><b>general purpose</b> e. g., word precessors, drawing tools
  like draw io
  <li><b>database-level</b> e. g., requirements management tools
  for organizing, storing retrieving and tracing requirements
  <li><b>language and method-based tools</b> e. g., tools
  supporting specific requirements languages e. g., drawing state
  machine diagrams or tools for specific methods like validation of
  models
</ul>
