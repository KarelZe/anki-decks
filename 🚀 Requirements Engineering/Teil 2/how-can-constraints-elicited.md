### Front
How can <b>constraints</b> elicited?

### Back
<ul>
  <li>Ask about restrictions of the potential solution space e. g.,
  technical, legal, organizational, cultural, environmental.
  <li>Check if a requirement is concealed behind a constraint e.
  g., "When in exploration mode, the print button must be grey."
  -> Actual requirement "When the system is used without a valid
  license, the system shall disable printing."
</ul>
