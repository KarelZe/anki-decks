## Front
How could a possible <b>change process</b> look like in
<b>requirements engineering</b>?

## Back
<ol>
  <li>Submit change request
  <li>Triage. Result: [OK | NO | Later (add to backlog)]
  <li>If OK: Impact analysis (4) Submit result and recommendation
  to Change Control Board
  <li>Decision by Change Control Board
  <li>If positive: make the change, create new baseline/release,
  (maybe) adapt the contract between customer and supplier
</ol>
