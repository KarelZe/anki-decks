## Front
What are the <b>elements</b> of a <b>model-based formal
specification</b>?

## Back
Mathematical model of system state and state change. Based on sets,
relations and logic expressions. Typical language elements include:
<ul>
  <li>base sets
  <li>relationshps (relations, functions)
  <li>invariants (predicates)
  <li>state changes (by relations or functions)
  <li>assertations
</ul>
