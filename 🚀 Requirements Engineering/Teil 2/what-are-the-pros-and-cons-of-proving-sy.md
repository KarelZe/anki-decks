### Front
What are the <b>pros</b> and <b>cons </b>of proving <b>system properties</b>?

### Back
<b>Pros:</b>
<ul><li>model checking is fully automatic and produces counter-examples in case of failure</li><li>exploring feasible subsets is a systematic, automated test</li></ul>
<b>Cons:</b>
<ul><li>classic proofs are still hard and labour-intensive</li><li>exploring the full state space is frequently infeasible</li></ul>
