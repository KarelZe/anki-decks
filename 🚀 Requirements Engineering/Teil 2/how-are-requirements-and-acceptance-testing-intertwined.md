## Front
How are <b>requirements</b> and <b>acceptance testing</b>
intertwined?

## Back
<ul>
  <li>For every requirement, there should be at least one
  acceptance test case.
  <li>Requirements must be written such that acceptance test can be
  written to validate them.
  <li>Acceptance tests can be used to disambiguating requirements
  and detailing specifications.
</ul>
