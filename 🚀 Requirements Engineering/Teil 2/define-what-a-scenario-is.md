### Front
Define what a <b>scenario </b>is.

### Back
<ol>
  <li>A description of a potential sequence of events that lead to
  a desired (or unwanted) result.
  <li>An ordered sequence of interactions between partners, in
  particular between a system and external actors. May be a
  concrete sequence (instance scenario) or a set of potential
  sequences (type scenario, use case).
</ol>
