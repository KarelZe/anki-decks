### Front
What are <b>typical reasons</b> for <b>conflicting
requirements</b>?

### Back
<ul>
  <li><b>Misinterpretation</b> (stakeholders interpret differently
  and disagree)
  <li><b>Subject matter conflict</b> (divergent factual needs)
  <li><b>Conflict of interest</b> (divergent interests, e.g. cost
  vs. function)
  <li><b>Conflict of value</b> (divergent values and preferences)
  <li><b>Relationship conflict</b> (emotional problems in personal
  relationships between stakeholders)
  <li><b>Organizational conflict</b> (between stakeholders on
  different hierarchy and decision power levels in an organization)
</ul>
