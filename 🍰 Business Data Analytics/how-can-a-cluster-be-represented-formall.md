### Front
How can a <b>cluster</b> be represented formally?

### Back
Each cluster \(C_{i}\) has a representative point \(\mu_{i}\).
Typically the mean.

Representative point is also known as centroid:
\[\mu_{i}=\frac{1}{|C|} \sum_{x_{j} \in C_{i}} x_{j}\]
