## Front
What is the variance inflation factor?

## Back
R-Squared of a predictor, if it is regressed by all other predictors.

\(\operatorname{VIF}\left(\hat{\beta}_{j}\right)=\frac{1}{1-R_{X_{j} / X_{-j}}^{2}}\)

Rule of thumb:
VIF of 5 or 10 is problematic.
