### Front
How is the cosine similarity defined? What is the intuition?

### Back
<b>Definition:</b>
\[\text { cosine similarity }=S_{C}(A, B):=\cos (\theta)=\frac{\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}}{\|\mathbf{A}\|\|\mathbf{B}\|}=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} A_{i} B_{i}}{\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n} A_{i}^{2}} \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n} B_{i}^{2}}},\]
where \(A_{i}\) and \(B_{i}\) are components of vector \(A\) and \(B\) respectively.

<b>Intuition:</b>
Correlation between objects (instead of variables)
