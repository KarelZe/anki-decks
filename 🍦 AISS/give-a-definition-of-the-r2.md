### Front
Give a <b>definition</b> of the \(R^2\).

### Back
\[R^{2}:=\frac{\text { Explained Variance }}{\text { Total Variance
}}=\frac{\sum_{j=1}^{n}\left(\hat{y}_{j}-\bar{y}_{j}\right)^{2}}{\sum_{j=1}^{n}\left(y_{j}-\bar{y}_{j}\right)^{2}}\]
