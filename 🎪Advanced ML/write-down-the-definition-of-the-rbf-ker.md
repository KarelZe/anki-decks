### Front
Write down the definition of the <b>rbf kernel.</b>

### Back
\[\mathrm{K}\left(\mathrm{x}, x_{i}\right)=\exp
\left\{-\gamma\left(\mathrm{x}-x_{i}\right)^{2}\right\},\]
<div>
  where \(\gamma\) is positive and \(\gamma\), \(r\) and \(d\) are
  kernel parameters.
</div>
