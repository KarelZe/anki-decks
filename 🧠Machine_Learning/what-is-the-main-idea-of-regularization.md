### Front
What is the main idea of <b>regularization</b>?

### Back
Limit the model such that it can not fit the training data perfectly anymore.<div>
</div><div>One introduces a <b>regularization penalty</b> in cost function:</div><div>\(E_{D}(\mathbf{w})+\lambda E_{W}(\mathbf{w}),\)
</div><div>
</div><div>where \(E_D\) is the error term from data, \(E_W\) is the regularization term and \(\lambda\) is the regularization factor.</div>
