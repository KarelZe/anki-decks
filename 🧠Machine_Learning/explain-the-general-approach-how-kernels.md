### Front
Explain the general approach how <b>kernels</b> can be used for
<b>density estimation</b>.

### Back
<b>Volume:</b><div>\(V=\int g(\boldsymbol{u}) d \boldsymbol{u}\)
</div><div>
</div><div><b>Summed kernel activation:</b></div><div>\(K\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{*}\right)=\sum_{i=1}^{N} g\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{*}-\boldsymbol{x}_{i}\right)\)
</div><div>
</div><div><b>Estimated density:</b></div><div>\(p\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{*}\right) \approx \frac{K\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{*}\right)}{N V} \equiv \frac{1}{N V} \sum_{i=1}^{N} g\left(\boldsymbol{x}_{*}-\boldsymbol{x}_{i}\right)\)
</div><div>
