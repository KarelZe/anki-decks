### Front
What is the difference between the mariginal distribution of a continous and a discrete latent variable?

### Back
Let \(\boldsymbol{x}\) be an observed variable and \(\boldsymbol{z}\) be an unobserved variable.<div>
</div><div>\(\underbrace{p(\boldsymbol{x} \mid \boldsymbol{\theta})=\sum_{z} p(\boldsymbol{x}, z \mid \boldsymbol{\theta})}_{\text {discrete latent variable }}, \quad \underbrace{p(\boldsymbol{x} \mid \boldsymbol{\theta})=\int_{\boldsymbol{z}} p(\boldsymbol{x}, \boldsymbol{z} \mid \boldsymbol{\theta}) d \boldsymbol{z}}_{\text {continuous latent variable }}\)
</div>
