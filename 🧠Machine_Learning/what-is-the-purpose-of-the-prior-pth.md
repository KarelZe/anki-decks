### Front
What is the purpose of the <b>Prior</b> \(p(\theta)\) in <b>Bayesian Learning</b>?<div>
</div><div>Also, give an example.</div>

### Back
The prior \(p(\theta)\) captures the belief and domain knowledge.<div>
</div><div>Example e. g. Gaussian prior:</div><div>\(p(\boldsymbol{\theta})=\mathcal{N}\left(\boldsymbol{\theta} \mid \mathbf{0}, \lambda^{-1} \boldsymbol{I}\right),\)
</div><div>
</div><div>where \(\lambda\) is the precision of the prior.</div>
