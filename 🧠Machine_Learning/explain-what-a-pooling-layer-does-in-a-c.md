### Front
Explain what a <b>pooling layer</b> does in a CNN.

### Back
Pooling layers make the representations smaller and more managable.<div>
</div><div>They do this by combining the outputs of neuron clusters at one layer into a single neuron in the next layer. The most popular way of combining is <b>max pooling</b>.</div><div>
</div><div>They operate over each activation map independently.</div><div>
