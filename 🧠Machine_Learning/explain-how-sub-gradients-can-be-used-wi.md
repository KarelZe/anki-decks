### Back

      <li>Initialize \(\boldsymbol{x}_{0}\)
      <li>Repeat: \(\boldsymbol{x}_{t+1}=\boldsymbol{x}_{t}+\eta
      \boldsymbol{g}\), where \(\boldsymbol{g}\) is any
      sub-gradient of \(f\) at point \(\boldsymbol{x}_{t}\).
