### Front
What do we optimize for in the <b>logistic regression</b> case?

### Back
<div>We optimize the cross-entropy loss given by:</div><div>
</div>\(\operatorname{argmax}_{\tilde{\boldsymbol{w}}} \log \operatorname{lik}(\tilde{\boldsymbol{w}}, D)=\operatorname{argmax}_{\tilde{\boldsymbol{w}}} \sum_{i} c_{i} \log \sigma\left(\tilde{\boldsymbol{w}}^{T} \tilde{\boldsymbol{x}}_{i}\right)+\left(1-c_{i}\right) \log \left(1-\sigma\left(\tilde{\boldsymbol{w}}^{T} \tilde{\boldsymbol{x}}_{i}\right)\right)\)
<div>
</div><div>Note that \(c_{i}\) hold the class labels. We try to find the best estitmates for \(\tilde{\boldsymbol{w}}\). </div>
