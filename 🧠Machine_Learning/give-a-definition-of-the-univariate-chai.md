### Front
Give a definition of the <b>univariate chain rule</b>.

### Back
If \(f(x)\) and \(x(t)\) are univariate functions, then
<div>
  \(\frac{d}{d t} f(x(t))=\frac{d f}{d x} \frac{d x}{d t}\)
</div>
