### Front
Name the <b>central moments</b>. (3 Pts)

### Back
<b>1st moment (mean):</b>
\(\boldsymbol{\mu}=\mathbb{E}_{p}[\boldsymbol{x}]\) <b>2nd
moment:</b> \(M_{2}=\mathbb{E}_{p}\left[\boldsymbol{x}
\boldsymbol{x}^{T}\right]\) <b>2nd central moment (covariance):</b>
\(\boldsymbol{\Sigma}=\mathbb{E}_{p}\left[(\boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{\mu})(\boldsymbol{x}-\boldsymbol{\mu})^{T}\right]\)
