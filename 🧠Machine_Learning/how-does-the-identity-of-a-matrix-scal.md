### Front
How does the <b>identity</b> of a matrix / scalar relate to its
<b>inverse</b>?

### Back
<div>
  <b>scalar case:</b>
</div>\(w \cdot w^{-1}=1\)
<div>
  <b>matrix case:</b>
</div>
<div>
  \(\boldsymbol{W} \boldsymbol{W}^{-1}=\boldsymbol{I}, \quad
  \boldsymbol{W}^{-1} \boldsymbol{W}=\boldsymbol{I}\)
</div>
