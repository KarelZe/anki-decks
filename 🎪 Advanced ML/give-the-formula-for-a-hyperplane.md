## Front
Give the <b>formula</b> for a <b>hyperplane</b>.

## Back
A hpyperplane in \(p\) dimensions is an affine subspace of
dimension \(p-1\).
<div>
  In general the equation for a hyperplane has the form:
</div>
<div>
  \[\beta_{0}+\beta_{1} X_{1}+\beta_{2} X_{2}+\ldots+\beta_{P}
  X_{P}=0\]
</div>
