x_{p}}}{1+e^{\beta_{0}+\beta_{1} X_{1}+\ldots+\beta_{p} x_{p}}}, \]
<div>

  number of predictors. \[\log
  \left(\frac{p(X)}{1-p(X)}\right)=\beta_{0}+\beta_{1}
  X_{1}+\ldots+\beta_{P} X_{P}\]
