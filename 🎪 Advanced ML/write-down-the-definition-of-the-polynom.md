### Front
Write down the definition of the <b>polynomial kernel</b>.

### Back
\[\mathrm{K}\left(x, x_{i}\right)=\left(\gamma x \cdot
x_{i}+r\right)^{d},\]
<div>
  where \(\gamma\) is positive and \(\gamma\), \(r\) and \(d\) are
  kernel parameters.
</div>
