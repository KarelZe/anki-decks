## Back
The probability of default for an indivual with balance of $1,000
is:
<div>
  \[\hat{p}(X)=\frac{e^{\hat{\beta}_{0}+\hat{\beta}_{1}
  X}}{1+e^{\hat{\beta}_{0}+\hat{\beta}_{1}
  X}}=\frac{e^{-10.6513+0.0055 \times 1,000}}{1+e^{-10.6513+0.0055
  \times 1,000}}=0.00576\]
</div>
