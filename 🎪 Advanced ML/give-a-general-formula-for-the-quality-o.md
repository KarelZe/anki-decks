### Front
Give a general formula for the quality of a split \(s\).

### Back
\[\Delta i(s, t)=i(t)-\pi(l) i(l)-\pi(r) i(r),\]
<div>
  where \(\pi(l)\) is the proportion of cases sent to the left by
  the split, and \(\pi(r)\) the proportion of cases sent to the
  right.
</div>
