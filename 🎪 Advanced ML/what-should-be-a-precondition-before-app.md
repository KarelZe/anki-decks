### Back

<div>
  \[\bar{x}_{i j}=\frac{x_{i j}}{\sqrt{\frac{1}{n}
  \sum_{i=1}^{n}\left(x_{i j}-\bar{x}_{j}\right)^{2}}}\]
</div>
