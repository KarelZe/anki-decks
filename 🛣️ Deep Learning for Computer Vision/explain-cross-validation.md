## Back
It's a resampling procedure used to evaluate ml models on a limited
data sample. Performed to avoid overfitting. A model is trained
using \(k-1\) of the folds as training data. The resulting model is
validated on the remaining fold. <b>Visualization:</b> <img src="paste-c3326e9561d07053c5ab7597a68af5e660704dce.jpg">
