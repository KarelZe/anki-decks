### Front
Give a definition and intuition behind the \(\ell_1\) and \(\ell_2\) loss

### Back
\(\ell_1\) -Loss (<b>Mean Average Error</b>): \[ L(x,
y)=\sum_{j}\left(y_{j}-x_{j}\right) \] \(\ell_2\) -Loss (<b>Mean
Square Error</b>): \[ L(x, y)=\sum_{j}\left(y_{j}-x_{j}\right)^{2}
\] Intuition: Predicting a one or multiple continuous quantities
\(\boldsymbol{y}_{1} \ldots \boldsymbol{y}_{n}\) Minimize the
distance between the predicted value \(\boldsymbol{x}_{j}\) true
values \(\boldsymbol{y}_{j}\)
