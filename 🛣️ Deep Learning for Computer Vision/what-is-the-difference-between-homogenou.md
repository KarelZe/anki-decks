### Front
What is the difference between <b>homogenous</b> and
<b>heterogenous ensembles</b>?

### Back
<ul>
  <li><b>Homogenous:</b> Same baselearner used multiple times e.g.,
  train on different data samples, different initializations,
  augmentations, regularizations etc.
  <li><b>Heterogenous:</b> Baselearner from different families. Low
  computation. Easier to train traditional models.
</ul>
